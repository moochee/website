Welcome to Moochee! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our interactive quiz game, Moochee. 1. Collection of Information

Personal Data

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering for hosting a quiz game, such as your name, email address. But we don't collect any personal information of players

We collect information related to your game participation, but only limited to the player nickname, scores for offering the history functionality.

Device and Access Information

When you access Moochee, we do not collect any information about your device and your access, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

QR Code Usage

When you scan the QR code to join a game, we do not collect information about your device and the time of scanning.
